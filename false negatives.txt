Rule Effects Write-Up
Types of rules with no etyma effected:
1. Partitioned/Recurrent:Partitionedrulewithnoeffects,butotherruleswithinthe set do have effects. There are about 35 of these.
2. Truenegative:Rule(thatisnotpartitionedorrecurrent)withnoeffectslistedin Hartmann and no effects in DiaCLESP. There are about 3 of these.
3. Falsenegative:Rule(thatisnotpartitionedorrecurrent)witheffectslistedin Hartmann but no effects DiaCLESP. There are about 7 of these, listed below.
